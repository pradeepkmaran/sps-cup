Overview

The Signal Processing Cup Challenge is a national-level innovation challenge organized by IEEE Madras Section and SSN College of Engineering, Chennai with five tracks: Biomedical Signal Processing : Brain Computer Interface (BCI), Wireless Sensing: Wi-Fi Vision, Environmental & Geospatial Signal Processing, Electronic Vehicles and Student Innovation.
